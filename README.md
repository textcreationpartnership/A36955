1. TO THE
RIGHT HONOURABLE
Arnold Ioost (i. e. Iustus) van Keppell,
Earl of ALBEMARLE, Viscount BURY,
Barron ASHFORD, of ASHFORD,
Master of the Robes to His Sacred Majesty,
And one of the
MAJOR-GENERALS of the KING's ARMIES.
